Our Story

Built on craft, not shortcuts.

Sky was founded on a simple standard: every service should feel considered, not rushed — and every client should leave looking like a better version of themselves, not a different one.

The Vision

Precision first. Trends second.

We built Sky around a belief that's easy to state and hard to practice: technical skill should always come before styling fashion. Colour formulas are mixed to the strand, not the season. Cuts are shaped to face and lifestyle before they're shaped to a reference photo.

That standard shapes everything downstream — the products we stock, the training our team commits to, and the amount of time we're willing to spend getting a single detail right.
